    About Us

    Prestige Lounge is a place that will bring true pleasure not only to connoisseurs of quality billiards, but also to lovers of Italian cuisine.
    The lounge is open to all professionals and billiards enthusiasts

    At your service are 2 American pool tables and 8 professional twelve-foot tables for playing Russian billiards, individual storage cabinets, and training in playing billiards.

    We are also pleased to offer you a cozy VIP room with a twelve-foot billiard table, accommodating up to 10 guests, which is perfect for events of any nature.
    The spacious and elegant interior will immerse you in a pleasant atmosphere, and the courteous and friendly staff will make your stay as comfortable as possible.
